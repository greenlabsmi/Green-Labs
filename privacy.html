Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Green Labs Provisions (“we,” “our,” “us”), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you provide while using our website and services.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: such as your name, phone number, email address, date of birth, and valid government-issued ID details (required for age verification, medical patient status, online ordering, or loyalty programs).
  • Usage Data: including browser type, IP address, and how you interact with our website.
  • Cookies & Tracking: small files used to improve website functionality, remember preferences, and enhance your shopping experience.

How We Use Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Process online orders and reserve products for in-store pickup.
  • Ensure strict compliance with Michigan Cannabis Regulatory Agency (CRA) laws, including verifying you are 21+ (or 18+ with a valid Michigan medical marijuana card).
  • Communicate with you regarding your orders, loyalty points, or store updates.
  • Send marketing promotions (only if you have explicitly opted in).

Information Sharing & State Compliance

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, as a licensed cannabis dispensary in the State of Michigan, we may be required to share certain transaction data with state regulatory bodies (such as through the METRC tracking system) to remain compliant with state laws. We may also share information with trusted third-party service providers (like our point-of-sale or menu embedding partners) solely to facilitate your orders.

Age Restriction

Our website and services are strictly intended for adults 21 years of age or older, or individuals 18+ who possess a valid Michigan medical marijuana patient registry card. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

Cookies

Cookies help us track preferences and improve our site. You may refuse cookies via your browser settings, but please note that some site functions (like keeping items in your cart) may not function properly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or state regulations. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
