Green Labs Guide

Not sure what to buy? Start here.

You do not need to know every strain, terpene, or product type to shop well. A few simple ideas can make your experience way better — and help you avoid buying the wrong thing.

Terpene Basics

What are terpenes?

If you have ever had one strain feel incredible and another feel completely off, terpenes are usually a big part of the reason why. They shape aroma, flavor, and often the kind of experience you actually have.

Terpenes are the natural aromatic compounds found in cannabis and many other plants. They are responsible for the smell and flavor of a strain, but they also help influence how that product tends to feel.

That is why two products with similar THC percentages can still create very different experiences. One may feel bright and social, while another may feel heavier, calmer, or more body-forward.

At Green Labs, many of our flower options — especially Dutch Touch Genetics — are terpene tested, which helps us guide people toward the kind of experience they are actually looking for.

Myrcene · relaxing Limonene · bright Pinene · clear Caryophyllene · grounding Linalool · calming

Tolerance + Timing

What is my dose?

There is no perfect number that works for everyone. Tolerance, mood, environment, food, and body chemistry all matter — which is why starting small is always the smartest move.

The honest answer is: everyone is different. Your ideal dose depends on tolerance, set and setting, what you have eaten, and how your body personally responds.

Start small. You can always take more. You can never take less.

Edible timing

Most edibles take 30–90 minutes to begin, peak around 2–3 hours, and can last 4–8 hours.

Food matters

Taking cannabis on a full stomach can often create a smoother and more predictable experience.

THC vs CBD

THC is the main intoxicating cannabinoid. CBD can help some people feel more balanced and less overwhelmed.

Best rule

Wait before taking more. Impatience is one of the biggest reasons people overdo it.

Budtender Guide

What should I say if I have no clue what I want?

That is completely normal. You do not need to know strain names, terpene charts, or percentages to shop well. If you can tell us how you want to feel, we can guide the rest.

The easiest thing to tell a budtender is simply how you want to feel: relaxed, creative, social, balanced, or ready for sleep. That gives us a real starting point.
